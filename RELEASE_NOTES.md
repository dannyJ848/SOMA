# Release Notes - The Biological Self v1.0.0

## First TestFlight Release

### Features
- 3D Anatomical Visualization with Z-Anatomy models
- Personal Health Data Tracking (encrypted, on-device)
- Patient Health Condition Visualization on 3D model
- AI Medical Encyclopedia with personalized education
- Voice Interface (Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech)
- Bilingual Support (English/Spanish)
- 5-Level Complexity System (8th grade to MD level)
- 2,700+ Educational Content Files

### Medical Simulation
- Symptom Explorer with anatomical source mapping
- Medication Explorer with physiology visualization
- Condition Simulator with disease progression
- Drug Interaction Checker

### Privacy
- All data stays on your device
- AES-256-GCM encryption
- Local AI processing via Ollama
- No cloud storage, no tracking

### Requirements
- iPhone 14 or later (iOS 17+)
- macOS 14+ for desktop

### Known Issues
- First launch may take a few seconds to load 3D models
- Voice commands work best in quiet environments
